to check acl
geftacl filename

to set facl
setfacl -m (modify) -u(username):<name-of-user>:<permission> filename

for group:
setfacl -m (modify) -g(group):<name-of-group>:<permission> filename

remove acl
setfacl -x u:username filename

remove all username
setfacl -b filename
